The Problem

Digital Poverty Is Economic Poverty

In the High Desert, talented people are being left behind because they lack devices, confidence with technology, and access to modern job pathways.

Facts and Statistics

0 Laptop

Many students and job-seekers share one outdated device or have none at all.

1 Barrier

No digital skills often means no access to remote, freelance, or tech-enabled jobs.

2 Outcomes

When people gain tools and training, they improve employability and income options.

Community Impact

A single parent should not have to choose between paying rent and buying a laptop for a child’s homework. A motivated young adult should not lose a job chance because they never learned to build a digital resume.
