Charity in Action: Helping People With Computers

HDDF mentors guide families and students through computer basics so they can access school, job training, and online opportunity.

HDDF creates guided learning spaces where people can sit down, ask questions, and start building confidence with technology.

Mentors and learners work side by side so computer training feels practical, personal, and connected to real opportunities.

Our programs support young adults and job-seekers who need both devices and community support to move forward.

Every session turns access into action by helping participants learn skills they can use immediately for school and work.
